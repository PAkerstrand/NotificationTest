Instructions on how to reset the notification permissions

Google Chrome
  1. Click the Chrome menu on the browser toolbar.
  2. Select Settings.
  3. Click Show advanced settings.
  4. In the "Privacy" section, click Content settings.
  5. In the dialog that appears, scroll down to the "Notifications" section.
  6. Click "Manage exceptions"
  7. Remove the entry originating from the host that displays this page
Safari
  1. Choose Safari > Preferences
  2. Click Notifications
  3. Select Allow or Deny for the website whose notification permission you want to change
Mozilla Firefox
  1. On the page with the permission granted, right click and select "Page Info".
  2. Click the "Permission" tab
  3. Under the "Show notifications" setting, click "Use default"
